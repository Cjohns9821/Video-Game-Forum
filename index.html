The Early Years (1970s–1980s)

Video games began as simple experiments in laboratories and arcades. Early titles like Pong and Space Invaders introduced the world to interactive digital entertainment.

These early games were limited by technology, but they laid the foundation for everything that followed.

Popular Early Games

  • Pong
  • Space Invaders
  • Pac-Man

Modern Video Games

Today’s games feature advanced graphics, online multiplayer, open worlds, and immersive storytelling. Modern gaming spans consoles, PCs, and mobile platforms, reaching millions of players worldwide.

From competitive esports to massive open-world adventures, modern gaming continues to push the boundaries of technology and creativity.
