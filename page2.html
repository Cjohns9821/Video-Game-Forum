The Rise of 3D and Online Play

By the late 1990s, consoles like the PlayStation and Nintendo 64 introduced fully 3D environments that transformed how games were designed and played.

Online gaming exploded in the 2000s, connecting millions of players across the world and creating entirely new genres. To this day, video games continue to gain traction, and are one of the stronger forms of creativity out there.
